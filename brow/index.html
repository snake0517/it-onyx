Beauty by Gabby, More Than a Massage

Beauty by Gabby is Little Rock's premier urban retreat day spa. Created with you in mind, The Remedy offers a spa experience that goes beyond the highest quality treatments and products. We are the place to relax, regroup, rejuvenate, and the ultimate escape from your busy life the second you walk in our doors. Our massages and services are designed for both men and women, The Remedy is a full-service spa, with well-appointed men’s and women’s locker rooms, private hot tubs, and a relaxation lounge stocked with all the amenities. The soak and sauna are available by private reservation only. We focus our attention on you in a cozy atmosphere and tailor treatments for your specific need.
