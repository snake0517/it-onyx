The Remedy Day Spa, More Than a Massage The Remedy is Albuquerque’s premier urban retreat day spa. Created with you in mind, The Remedy offers a spa experience that goes beyond the highest quality treatments and products. We are the place to relax, regroup, rejuvenate, and the ultimate escape from your busy life the second you walk in our doors. Our massages and services are designed for both men and women, The Remedy is a full-service spa, with well-appointed men’s and women’s locker rooms, private hot tubs, and a relaxation lounge stocked with all the amenities. The soak and sauna are available by private reservation only. We focus our attention on you in a cozy atmosphere and tailor treatments for your specific need.

Marine Algae and Chamomile Body Wraps Full body exfoliation to prep your skin for a marine algae and chamomile masque that is an antioxidant infused blend of highly potent, fortifying algae and essential oils seep into the undernourished skin to renew strength and vitality. Lavender essential oil, chamomile, Marigold, Lime and cornflower extracts combined with Marine algae, kelp and glycerin to soothe and heal skin, leaving it soft and refreshed. Session is finished with swaddle-like wrapping of the body to promote relaxation and scalp massage while treatment sets.
